Here are information about C.U.O.N., an ERP system for Linux, and (with some restrictions) Windows that is completely under the GPL V3. Here's German version , and the original English version of the GPL V3.
This is the topically state of the project:



CUON based in the server area at PYTHON and POSTGRES, currently in the client area exists a client in Python with GTK (Glade).
Among the normal work processes, such as addresses, order, articles and more. There is a Document Management System (DMS), which many formats (Office documents, images, audio and video). Also it is very comfortable to manage the full correspondence with OpenOffice .

To the Wiki and Bug-Trackung-System go here.

The Report Server:
