Chapter 1. Installation

This side describes the general installation of cuon. Please see also

1.1. Requirements

The server needs some resources. So it should look approximately in such a way

  • servers and 1 Client on the same computer starting from 3GHz, 1GB RAM, 10 GB HD

  • servers without GUI and 10 Clients starting from 2.5 GHz, 1 GB RAM, 100 GB HD

  • servers without GUI and 50 Clients starting from 3 GHz, 4 GB RAM, 500 GB HD

Client computer must have a GUI like Gnome, KDE, XFCE That Install client must be a Linux computer . X must run and a surface such as XFCE, Gnomes, KDE, black box, more windowmaker or another surface must be present.
